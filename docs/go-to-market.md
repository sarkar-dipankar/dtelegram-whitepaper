# Go-to-Market Strategy

This document outlines dTelecom's comprehensive go-to-market strategy, including customer acquisition, marketing initiatives, partnerships, and growth tactics across all project phases.

## Market Positioning

**Value Proposition**: dTelecom provides decentralized, secure, and cost-effective real-time communication infrastructure that saves businesses up to 95% compared to centralized alternatives while ensuring data sovereignty and superior reliability.
