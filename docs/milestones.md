# Project Milestones & Deliverables

This document outlines the key milestones, deliverables, and success criteria for the dTelecom project across all phases of development.

## Milestone Overview

Each milestone represents a significant achievement or deliverable that moves the project forward. Milestones are organized by phase and include clear success criteria.

## Success Dashboard

### Overall Project Health Indicators

```yaml
Green (On Track):
  - All milestones within 1 month of target
  - Budget within 10% of plan
  - Team retention >90%
  - Customer satisfaction >4.0/5

Yellow (Attention Needed):
  - Milestones 1-2 months delayed
  - Budget 10-20% over
  - Team retention 80-90%
  - Customer satisfaction 3.5-4.0/5

Red (Intervention Required):
  - Milestones >2 months delayed
  - Budget >20% over
  - Team retention <80%
  - Customer satisfaction <3.5/5
```

## Risk Tracking

Each milestone includes risk assessment:

**Risk Categories**
1. **Technical Risk** - Technology challenges
2. **Market Risk** - Market conditions, competition
3. **Regulatory Risk** - Legal/compliance issues
4. **Execution Risk** - Team capability, resources
5. **Financial Risk** - Funding, burn rate

**Mitigation Requirements**
- Each milestone must have identified risks
- Mitigation plans documented
- Regular risk reviews (monthly)
- Contingency plans for critical milestones
